Energy Wastage in Traditional Systems

The Challenge

Traditional energy systems suffer from significant inefficiencies. In areas like Gilutongan Island, residents experience power instability while massive amounts of kinetic energy from daily foot traffic go untapped. SmartStep was born to capture this lost potential.

The SmartStep Innovation

Using the direct piezoelectric effect, we convert mechanical stress from footsteps directly into clean electricity. Our IoT integration allows for real-time monitoring and smart power distribution across remote infrastructure.
