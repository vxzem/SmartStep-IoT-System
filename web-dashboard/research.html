General Objective

To design and implement a scalable, IoT-integrated piezoelectric energy harvesting system capable of reducing energy wastage in off-grid Philippine communities.

Specific Objectives

  • Develop a **modular PZT array** for easy tile-based installation.
  • Implement an **intelligent BMS** to manage high-frequency voltage spikes.
  • Integrate **IoT real-time monitoring** for remote diagnostics on Gilutongan Island.

Scope & Limitations

The current SmartStep prototype is optimized for **low-current applications** (LED lighting, mobile charging, and small IoT sensors). It is currently **not designed for high-wattage devices** such as laptops or power tools due to the current storage capacity of the battery modules. System reporting is dependent on local network availability for IoT sync.
